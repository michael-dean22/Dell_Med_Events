How to Subscribe

Subscribe once and all Dell Med events appear automatically in your Outlook calendar — updated daily.

1
Copy the subscribe URL
Click Copy Link at the top of this page.
2
Open Account Settings
In Outlook, go to File → Account Settings → Account Settings
3
Add Internet Calendar
Click the Internet Calendars tab → New → paste the URL → click Add
4
Done!
Events appear in your calendar under Other Calendars. Outlook syncs automatically.
1
Copy the subscribe URL
Click Copy Link at the top of this page.
2
Open Outlook Calendar
Go to outlook.office.com and click the Calendar icon.
3
Add Calendar
Click Add calendar → Subscribe from web → paste the URL → click Import
4
Done!
