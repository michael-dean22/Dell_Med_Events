How to Subscribe

Subscribe once and all Dell Med events appear automatically in your Outlook — updated daily.

1
Copy the subscribe URL
Click Copy Link at the top of this page.
2
Open Account Settings
In Outlook, go to File → Account Settings → Account Settings
3
Add Internet Calendar
Click Internet Calendars tab → New → paste URL → click Add
4
Done!
Events appear under Other Calendars. Syncs automatically.

3

4
Done!
